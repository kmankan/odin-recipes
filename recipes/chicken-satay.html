~ Delicious Chicken Satay ~

Description

Indulge in the mouthwatering flavors of this Delicious Chicken Satay recipe! Tender chicken thighs are marinated in a rich and creamy blend of coconut milk, peanut butter, and aromatic spices, then seared to perfection. The accompanying homemade peanut sauce adds a delightful depth of flavor, making this dish an irresistible treat for any occasion. Whether you're hosting a dinner party or simply looking for a satisfying meal, this Chicken Satay is sure to impress with its bold and authentic taste.

Ingredients

  • 6 chicken thighs (bone in)
  • 6 tablespoons coconut milk (from a 400ml tin)
  • 2 1/4 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoon packed light brown sugar (omit for KETO)
  • 2 tablespoon Thai red curry paste
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon Kecap Manis (sweet soy sauce)
  • 1 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)
  • 2 cup coconut milk
  • 1/2 cup peanut butter
  • 1 1/2 tbs brown sugar
  • 1 1/2 tbs curry paste
  • 1 1/2 tbs sweet soy sauce
  • 0.75 tamarind paste (optional)
  • 1 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
  • Salt
  • 1.5 -3 tablespoons lime juice

Steps

  1. Combine the following ingredients in a large, shallow bowl until well combined and creamy:
    • 6 tablespoons coconut milk (from a 400ml tin)
    • 2 1/4 tablespoons creamy peanut butter
    • 2 tablespoon packed light brown sugar (omit for KETO)
    • 2 tablespoon Thai red curry paste
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon Kecap Manis (sweet soy sauce)
    • 1 1/2 tablespoon fish sauce (optional)
  2. Add in the 6 chicken thighs (bone in), turning to coat completely with the marinade. Allow to marinade for at least 10 minutes to half an hour in the refrigerator.
  3. Preheat oven to 400°F | 200°C.
  4. Heat oil in an oven-proof pan or skillet over medium-high heat. Sear the chicken until browned on each side and fragrant (about 8-10 minutes each side). Transfer to the oven and continue cooking for a further 30 minutes, or until the chicken is cooked through.
  5. While the chicken is in the oven, combine all Peanut Sauce ingredients into a saucepan EXCEPT for the 1.5 -3 tablespoons lime juice:
    • 2 cup coconut milk
    • 1/2 cup peanut butter
    • 1 1/2 tbs brown sugar
    • 1 1/2 tbs curry paste
    • 1 1/2 tbs sweet soy sauce
    • 1 1/2 tbs fish sauce (optional)
    • 0.75 tamarind paste (optional)
    • 1 1/2 teaspoon minced garlic
    • salt
  6. Bring to a boil, reduce heat and allow to simmer until combined and thickened (about 5-6 minutes). Once thick, take off heat and stir in the lime juice (start with 1 tablespoon and adjust accordingly to suit your tastes).
  7. Serve the chicken with the peanut sauce: either pour it all into the pan with the chicken OR serve separately.
